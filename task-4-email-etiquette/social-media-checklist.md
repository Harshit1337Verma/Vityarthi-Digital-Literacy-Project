### Part-B: Social Media Responsibility Checklist

| Do's | Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| Keep Auditing your privacy settings time to time to keep track of  who can see your personal posts. | Post personal or important details like user ID,  address, or any other private project details. |
| Use a professional profile picture with and clear bio (which includes professional gmail id username and project details) on LinkedIn to build a positive brand. | Engage in "cyber-venting" or posting unnecessary details or contradicting details, Prevent from giving over information for eg. Putting many different coding languages while having mastery in onyl one or two languages. |
| Verify information before sharing to prevent the spread of harmful rumors. | Plagiarize or share copyrighted material without proper attribution and verification. |
| Network with relevant industry professionals and alumnis in relevant tech groups. | Post content depicting illegal activities or violations related to university policies . |
| Always think before you tag others and always remember to respect the privacy of your friends and classmates. | Assume that "deleted" content is permanently delted from the internet and no other copies are there. |
